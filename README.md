📊 Manager Sales Dashboard – Excel / Dashboard Project


I created a Manager Sales Dashboard to analyze sales performance by managers/supervisors across different product categories, brands, and states.
The main goal of this project was to convert raw sales data into an interactive dashboard and track:

Sales performance
Quantity sold
Total cost
Order performance
Brand contribution
State-wise sales
Manager/Supervisor performance

This dashboard gives a complete view of business performance in one place and helps management make quick decisions.

📊 1. KPI (Key Performance Indicators) Used

💰 Total Sales
Shows total revenue generated from all products.

Why used:
Helps track overall business growth.

📦 Total Quantity Sold
Displays total number of products sold.

Why used:
Measures demand and sales volume.

💸 Total Cost
Shows total cost spent on products.

Why used:
Helps compare spending with sales.

🧾 Total Orders
Displays number of customer orders.

Why used:
Tracks order activity and performance.

📈 Total Profit / Cost Analysis
Used to compare sales vs cost.

Why used:
Helps identify profitability.

👨‍💼 Supervisor / Manager Performance
Shows performance of each manager.

Why used:
Helps compare team performance and productivity.
